What is Shoestrap?

Shoestrap is a simple Python-based alternative to Chef and Puppet. It speaks Python so there's virtually no learning curve. This is a fork of https://github.com/cmer/shoestrap, because I don't like bash.

You should be able to get up and running in minutes, not hours.

What's wrong with Chef, Puppet and co.?

Chef and Puppet are great tools, but they are too complex for most use cases. The learning curve for these tools is quite steep as they each have their own DSL. On the other end, Shoestrap is just Python. It does not require any 'Python to config files' translation.

I believe Shoestrap is a great simple alternative to Chef or Puppet that will fulfill the needs of most people.
